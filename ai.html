Texts & Images

Why AI in Sentiment?

AI goes beyond just classification: it reshapes how businesses, politics, and individuals interpret emotions hidden in text, images, and even speech. Proper design of these systems impacts brand reputation, market research, and even policymaking.



Elements That Matter

Every element: datasets, preprocessing, architectures, & model choice, critically affects the outcome. For example, imbalanced datasets skew predictions, while fusion methods (early, late, attention-based) decide how well text and images complement each other.



How Design Shapes Results

Different models excel in different areas:
• BERT handles nuanced text sentiment.
• VGG-Face/FaceNet extract emotions from images.
• CLIP aligns image–text understanding for zero-shot tasks. This system design is about combining them effectively for interpretability, speed, and accuracy.



Problem Solving with Multimodal AI

AI uncovers connections humans often miss:
• Detecting subtle sarcasm in text.
• Recognizing mixed emotions (anger + fear) in the same content.
• Bridging visual cues with textual tone for complete sentiment context.
This integration makes AI not just analytical but predictive and preventive in real-world crises.



Future Horizons

Next steps involve integrating audio (tone of voice) and video (spatio-temporal emotion tracking). These will unlock mental health monitoring, deeper consumer personalization, and context-aware AI.
